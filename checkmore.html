About

Approach:
The enormous Banquets is only 12 mins away from the Railway Station and only 30 mins from the Mumbai International Airport. Lohallsed on Link Road and close to Mindspace, the venue is easily approachable.


Entrance and Outside:
Situated on the 2nd and 5th floor of the famous mall, the Banquets has the advantage of being close to many restaurants and shopping stores. The simple, five-storeyed mall houses the halls.


Ambiance:
The tiny, circular lights along with the structured chandeliers and the wooden panels add finesse to any event. The classic chairs and the patterned carpets are the perfect decorations to an already perfect room. banquet offers 9 spacious banquets which can hallser to many occasions. 10 to 1200 guests can be accommodated as per the size of a party.


Food and Services:
The patrons can select from Indian, Chinese, Thai, Lebanese as well as Continental cuisines to go with a large variety of cocktails and sparkling drinks. The live counters are a must try! Other services provided in Banquets are valet parking, in-house decoration, audio-visual equipment and DJ music.


Why you should choose this venue:
The many halls, the zesty food, the lohallsion is what makes the Banquets ideal for any function.
